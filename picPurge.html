Welcome to the PicPurge support website
PicPurge allows you to effortlessly sift through your smartphone’s gallery, swiping away unnecessary photos to declutter your space. But don’t worry about losing anything important - swiped photos aren't permanently deleted. They're moved to another view where you can review them. Here, decide to either restore mistakenly swiped images or confirm their deletion for good. Manage your photo collection easily and keep only what matters.
